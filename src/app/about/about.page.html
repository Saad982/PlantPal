About PlantPal About PlantPal

This project, PlantPal, is developed as part of my Web and Mobile Assignment. The main purpose of this app is to help users capture images of plants and record their exact location using geolocation.

In this project, I have integrated various modules to provide a complete plant care experience:

Geolocation module to find your current location. Camera module to capture images of plants for your records. Plant Library with detailed plant information and images. Watering Schedule to remind you when to water your plants. Care Tips section with interactive plant care advice.

This project is built using Ionic Framework and Angular, offering both web and mobile support.
